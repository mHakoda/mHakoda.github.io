About

Interesting tool with which you can create your own web page with a digital business card of all your contacts.

Task

A virtual business card service where all contacts are collected in one link in a convenient and beautiful way.

Description

Since I have some idea of design, I developed my own design for this project. Later I went to the code, developed a landing page where I showed all the strengths of the project, studied the experience of competitors and made a small but informative page with prices and appeals to the three groups of audience for which this startup was designed.

I want to say a few words about who this project was created for. First of all for you, create a personal business card for yourself with all your contacts. For freelancers, your portfolio and contacts for communication, all in one convenient place for your customers. For businesses, plug your business card into your company website for easy communication with your business.

Next, it was time to take care of the admin. This is where each user can set up his own business card, depending on his needs. There you can choose the page background, company logo or profile picture, fill in the title and description, customize the main button and add links to social networks. Customization is done from the sidebar and everything is displayed in real time on the screen.

At the end, the user will get a link to a web page with his business card, which he can place anywhere and all his contact information will now be collected in one, convenient and beautiful place.
